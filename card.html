Eastern Grey Squirrel

Squirrels most often have slender bodies with a very long and bushy tail.

  • Scientific Name: Sciurus carolinensis
  • Average Weight: Varies between 400-600g
  • Average Lifespan: 1-2 years
  • Habitat:Mature dense woodland

Eastern Grey Squirrels love to eat a variety of nuts, seeds, berries, flowers and buds. Grey Squirrels have an excellent sense of smell. They communicate with eachother by making sounds and body movements, such as tail flicking. Grey squirrels can swim and hang upside down. They can also be right or left handed!
